About Ganpati Marbles

Ganpati Marbles was born back in 1985 to decorate every space with majesty, finesse and supreme quality, staying true to the principles this company was founded on. Ganpati Marbles has consistently brought and will continue to bring the largest volumes of the finest Statuario Marble. With our amazing hospitality, affordable prices and quality products we make the customer ours forever.

The Founder, Deepak Surana has built a legacy of unmatched quality since 1985, and is today recognized for Banswara and exquisite Statuario marble. Our assorted variety of Products includes Makrana Dungri Marble, Granite, Natural Stones, Katni Marble, Sandstone and so on. The team along with Mr Manoj Bothra strives hard to deliver consignments safely and within a stipulated period to our valued clients.
